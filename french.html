Parlez-vous français?

Discover the language of diplomacy, culture, and opportunity. Join millions of French speakers worldwide.

Why Learn French?

Global Language

French is spoken by over 300 million people across five continents and is an official language in 29 countries.

Career Opportunities

Enhance your career prospects in international business, diplomacy, fashion, culinary arts, and tourism.

Cultural Gateway

Access French cinema, literature, art, and cuisine in their original form and understand French culture deeply.

French Language Levels

DELF A1 - Beginner

Basic communication skills for everyday situations:

  • Introduce yourself and others
  • Ask and answer simple questions
  • Basic vocabulary and expressions

DELF A2 - Elementary

Handle simple, routine exchanges:

  • Describe daily routines and preferences
  • Basic social interactions
  • Simple past and future tenses

Official French Examinations

DELF

Diplôme d'études en langue française - Official certification for French proficiency at A1 to B2 levels.

DALF

Diplôme approfondi de langue française - Advanced certification for C1 and C2 levels.

TCF

Test de connaissance du français - French proficiency test for academic and professional purposes.
